TOURISM

3-2 and 4-2

Tourism students in third and fourth form study the travel and hospitality industry, focusing on its economic impact, types of tourism, and customer service. As they advance, they explore sustainable tourism, marketing strategies, transportation systems, and the role of tourism organizations. This prepares them for exams and careers in tourism, hospitality, and related fields.
