This course provides students with fundamental knowledge in Technical Drawing, Woodwork, and Mechanical Engineering. It aims to develop practical skills while emphasizing the significance of the vocational sector in Belize. Through hands-on learning, students gain an understanding of industry practices, preparing them for future careers in technical and skilled trades.

Student practicals for this class include:

Welding Practicals

B&FT Practicals

TD Drawings and Scaled Model of Infrastructure
