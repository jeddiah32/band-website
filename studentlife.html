STUDENT LIFE

Lower Division

First Form

First-form students in secondary school are in their first year of high school, usually around ages 11–13. This is a transition period where they adjust to a new learning environment, build foundational knowledge in core subjects like Math, English, Science, and Social Studies, and develop important study and organizational skills. It's a time for exploration, making new friends, and laying the groundwork for future academic success.

First Form General Subjects

Major Subjects

* Life Skills

* Spanish

* Agriculture

* Physical Education

* Belizean Studies

Second Form

Second-form students in secondary school are typically in their second year of high school, around ages 12–14. At this stage, they build on the foundation from first form, studying core subjects like Math, English, Science, and Social Studies while developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
